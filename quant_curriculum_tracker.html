Month 1: Market Mechanics

What is SciML to Quant Finance?

This curriculum bridges Scientific Machine Learning (SciML) — the discipline of applying physics-informed AI to complex dynamical systems — with Quantitative Finance. Markets are dynamical systems governed by order flow, inventory, and risk dynamics. By learning to build models from first principles (like physicists) and validate them rigorously (like engineers), you develop the technical foundation to systematically trade and research financial markets.

Prerequisites

You should be comfortable with: Python (pandas, numpy, scipy), calculus and linear algebra, basic probability and statistics, and willingness to read research papers. You do not need prior finance knowledge — the curriculum teaches that from the ground up.

Learning Trajectory

Months 1–2: Build your mental model of markets as data, distributions, and dynamical systems.
Months 3–4: Learn the mathematics of pricing and the mechanics of execution.
Months 5–6: Apply machine learning and AI with proper validation and production rigor.
